Support

Get help with ScreenMark for Mac.

System Requirements

ScreenMark requires macOS 15.0 (Sequoia) or later on a Mac. Apple Silicon and Intel Macs are both supported.

Permissions

ScreenMark needs Screen Recording permission to function. Go to System Settings → Privacy & Security → Screen Recording and enable ScreenMark. Restart the app after granting permission.

Frequently Asked Questions

The overlay doesn't appear — what do I do?
Check that Screen Recording permission is enabled in System Settings and that ScreenMark is running in your menu bar. Try quitting and relaunching the app.
How do I activate annotation mode?
Press the global shortcut (default: ⌘⌥A) from any app. The toolbar will appear at the edge of your screen.
My purchase isn't restoring.
Open ScreenMark → Settings → Subscription and tap Restore Purchases. Make sure you're signed in to the same Apple ID used to purchase. If the issue persists, contact us.
How do I cancel my subscription?
Open System Settings → Apple ID → Subscriptions and cancel ScreenMark Pro from there. You'll retain access until the current billing period ends.
Where are my exported files saved?
Exports default to your Desktop. You can change the export location in ScreenMark Settings.
