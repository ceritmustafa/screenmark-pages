Privacy policy

ScreenMark keeps presentation work on your Mac.

This privacy policy applies to ScreenMark for macOS. The app is designed to work locally: no ScreenMark account is required, and the app does not upload your presentation content to a ScreenMark-operated server.

Information ScreenMark handles

  • Local settings and diagnostics

    Preferences, entitlement cache, and local diagnostic events are stored on your Mac to support the app experience.

  • Screen and audio data

    Screen, microphone, and system audio data are used only when you activate capture features such as zoom, freeze, snip, or recording.

  • App Store purchase data

    Subscriptions and purchases are processed by Apple through StoreKit. ScreenMark reads entitlement state from the App Store to unlock paid features.

What ScreenMark does not do

  • No user account

    You do not need to sign in to use ScreenMark.

  • No ScreenMark cloud upload

    The app does not send your screen content, recordings, annotations, or exports to a ScreenMark server.

  • No keystroke logging

    Accessibility or input monitoring access is used for shortcut handling only and is not used to record typed content.

Permissions

Screen Recording is required for capture features. Microphone access is required only if you choose to record microphone input.

Retention

Files you export or record stay wherever you save them on your Mac unless you move them elsewhere.

Contact

Privacy questions can be sent to apple@mustafacerit.com.
