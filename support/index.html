Support

Help for setup, permissions, and purchases.

ScreenMark runs locally on your Mac and depends on macOS permissions for screen capture, microphone capture, and global shortcut behavior. This page is the public support URL for the Mac App Store release.

Permissions

  • Screen Recording

    Required for zoom, freeze frame, screen recording, and snapshot export. ScreenMark does not capture anything until you activate a feature.

  • Microphone

    Required only when you enable microphone capture during a recording session.

  • Accessibility or Input Monitoring

    Used for global hotkeys and presentation controls so the overlay can react while you work in another app.

Purchases and restore

  • Introductory offer

    Eligible new subscribers can start with a 7-day App Store trial on monthly or yearly plans.

  • Restore purchases

    Open the Purchases pane in ScreenMark Settings and choose Restore Purchases.

  • Lifetime unlock

    The lifetime option is a separate one-time App Store purchase and does not require a subscription.

Troubleshooting checklist

If zoom or recording does not start, confirm Screen Recording permission and relaunch the app after granting access in macOS Settings.

Recording notes

GIF recording is silent by design. For microphone or system audio, record to MP4 or MOV and verify the audio toggles before you start.

Contact

Email apple@mustafacerit.com for store-review questions, support requests, or account-side purchase issues.
