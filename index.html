Features

Everything you need to present with confidence

ScreenMark sits invisibly over every app, activating instantly with global shortcuts — no window switching, no focus stealing.

🎨

Draw on Screen

Annotate anything live with a natural pen, shapes, arrows, and text. Pick from a rich color palette. Undo, redo, or clear with a single keystroke.

❄️

Freeze & Capture

Freeze the entire display mid-presentation to annotate a static snapshot. Export as PDF, PNG, or TIFF — perfect for archiving your annotated moments.

⬜

Instant Whiteboard

Launch a blank whiteboard in one keystroke. Pair it with a built-in countdown timer (5, 10, or 15 minutes) for group activities and timed exercises.

🎥

Screen Recording

Record your screen with live annotations baked in. Export as video or GIF. Everything you draw, highlight, and annotate appears in the final recording.

⌨️

Global Shortcuts

Every action is one keystroke away — system-wide, no matter which app is in focus. Customize hotkeys to match your personal workflow.
