Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2025

No Data Collected

ScreenMark does not collect, transmit, or store any personal data. All screen content, annotations, recordings, and exports stay entirely on your device and in your personal iCloud account.

Screen Recording Permission

ScreenMark requests macOS Screen Recording permission to display your screen content in the overlay and in recordings. This content is never sent off your device.

iCloud Sync

If you enable iCloud sync, your settings and exported files are stored in your personal iCloud Drive. ScreenMark has no access to your iCloud data — Apple's standard iCloud privacy terms apply.

In-App Purchases

Purchases are processed entirely by Apple via StoreKit. ScreenMark never handles or stores payment information. Apple's privacy policy governs purchase data.

Diagnostics (Optional)

If you opt in to Apple's app analytics sharing in macOS System Settings, Apple may share anonymised crash reports and usage statistics with us. This is optional and can be disabled at any time in System Settings → Privacy & Security → Analytics & Improvements.

Third-Party SDKs

ScreenMark contains no third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking SDKs.

Children's Privacy

ScreenMark does not collect data from any user, including children. The app is rated 4+.

Contact

Questions about privacy? Email us: apple@mustafacerit.com
